Welcome to the AOT

AOT an animation known for its intense action and drama. In terms of animation, "Attack on Titan" is a 2D hand-drawn anime that utilizes both traditional animation techniques and computer-generated graphics to create its dynamic and often brutal fight scenes. The show is also notable for its use of dynamic camera angles and swift editing to heighten the sense of tension and excitement during action sequences. Even though it seems like typical another Japanese animation, it is not. This animation contains many different aspects of human beings such as love, anger, will to become a free man, cruelty, peace, war, and ambitions. While watching this animation, it will make you feel like you are watching a human history such as World War. If you are ready to learn more about AOT, let's get started.
